Product Info

Trouble & Trix Ultra-Scoop Cat Litter is the litter I often use for my cat. This litter comes in a refreshing Natural scent. Features carbon that highly absorbs and traps odour, a zeolite that locks in urine smells and baking soda that neutralises odours. This natural and 100% biodegradable litter forms an easy to clean, provides a sense of calm and stability to a fresh home. It is a perfect cat litter for your cozy home!

List of benefits

  • Made from sustainable and eco-friendly materials, is biodegradable and compostable.
  • Excellent absorbency and odor control with natural clumping properties.
  • Gentle on cat's paws, and won't cause discomfort or irritation.
  • A variety of textures and formulas, including pellet, paper, and clumping varieties.
