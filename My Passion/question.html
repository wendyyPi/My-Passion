My Reflection

Through my experience with website design, I have gained a comprehensive understanding of the design process. I learned that creating a website involves first writing the HTML code, then adding CSS for style and design, and finally using JavaScript to add interactivity and functionality. To illustrate, HTML serves as the basic structure of the website, like a human with basic head, body and foot. While CSS is similar to the human clothing or makeup that enhance appearance and make the human looks more nice, JavaScript functions like actions that make a person more dynamic, like smiling, running, or jumping. Also, I think website design is an iterative process and require continuous improvement. when design my website, I begin by creating a rough website and then continuously enhance it by adding or modifying elements to make it more visually appealing. Additionally, I found it helpful to test my website with friends to obtain feedback, allowing me to identify areas for improvement and enhance its usability. One important lesson I learned is the value of planning carefully from the outset. If I could go back in time, I would prototype the entire website layout and color scheme before coding. As Initially, I just find some basic website wireframe online and start to code right away. Then during the coding process, I find it is hard to organise various parts and I spend more time adjusting my website which was inefficient. To avoid this in the future, I have learned to prioritize planning before beginning the website design process.

Common Cat Health Issues and Treatment

Urinary tract infections (UTIs) symptoms include frequent urination, painful urination, and blood in the urine. Treatment involves clearing Obstructions in the urethra and using antibiotics prescribed and providing clean drinking water and a high-quality diet.

Dental disease symptoms include bad breath, difficulty eating, and loose or missing teeth. Treatment involves using dental cleaning and regular tooth brushing to remove plaque from the teeth, and in extreme cases, removing the teeth that may serve as sources of inflammation.

Hairballs symptoms include vomiting, lack of appetite, and constipation. Treatment involves hairball medication like add fiber or laxative and try cat food formulated for hairballs and groom cat regularly.

Fleas symptoms include excessive scratching and biting, and small black flecks on the skin or fur. Treatment involves try oral flea medication and over-the-counter anti-itch creams. Also cleaning the environment deeply to avoid fleas happen again.

Worms symptoms include vomiting, diarrhea, distended abdomen , weight loss/unhealthy appearance Treatment involves use of heartworm, intestinal worm, and parasite prevention and good hygiene practices. Bayer Drontal Broad Spectrum Dewormer is a good worm medication.
